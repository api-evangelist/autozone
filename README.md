# AutoZone (autozone)

AutoZone is the nation's leading retailer and a leading distributor of automotive replacement parts and accessories, operating more than 7,000 stores across the United States, Mexico, and Brazil. The company provides auto parts, maintenance items, and accessories to both do-it-yourself customers and professional service providers through its AutoZone Pro program.

## APIs

### AutoZone Pro Electronic Ordering
AutoZone Pro provides electronic ordering capabilities for professional automotive service providers, enabling parts lookup and ordering through EDI-based integrations. AutoZone does not currently offer a public REST API; integrations are handled through EDI and third-party partners.

**Human URL:** [https://www.autozonepro.com/info/about/Electronic-Ordering.jsp](https://www.autozonepro.com/info/about/Electronic-Ordering.jsp)
